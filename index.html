Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an entrance exam?

Yes, for select programs. See dates above. National-level scores (e.g., JEE, GATE) may be considered where applicable.

Can I apply before getting my final results?

Yes. You can apply with predicted/previous semester scores and upload final marks later.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Yes, separate hostels for boys and girls with limited seats allotted on first‑come basis.

How can I track my application?

After submission, you will receive an email with a secure portal link to track your status.
